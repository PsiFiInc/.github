# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

PsiFi Inc. is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who interacts with our organization, repositories, and community.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community and users
- Show empathy towards others

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all PsiFi spaces, including our GitHub repositories, community forums, social media, and any other channels where PsiFi is represented.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to conduct@psifi.app. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
